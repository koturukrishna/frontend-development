About

Aurora Technological Institute (Telangana), is a part of Aurora Group of Institutions, which are functioning under the aegis of Aurora group of Engineering Colleges. Mr Ramesh Nimmatoori, a Post Graduate Degree in Computer Science Engineering, who has struggled to get the quality education wanted to ensure that no one else would face the hardships faced by him. It is this idea that gave birth to Aurora’s Group of Institutions. From humble beginnings in the year 1989, the group has grown and has diversified into the fields of Engineering, Management, Life Sciences, Commerce and Physical Sciences Education. Aurora’s Engineering Colleges have been established with the aim to provide quality undergraduate and post graduate engineering education in the state of Andhra Pradesh.

ATI Engineering College Course Offerings

Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech)
  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
    • Focus: Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Analytics.
    • Career Opportunities: Software Engineer, Data Scientist, AI Specialist.
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
    • Focus: IoT, Embedded Systems, and Wireless Communication.
    • Career Opportunities: Communication Engineer, VLSI Designer, IoT Specialist.
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
    • Focus: Robotics, Design Engineering, and Thermal Sciences.
    • Career Opportunities: Design Engineer, Manufacturing Specialist, Automotive Engineer.
Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech)
